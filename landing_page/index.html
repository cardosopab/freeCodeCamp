Personalized Schedules

With the Hobbit Pet Feeder, each pet receives a customized feeding schedule based on their specific needs. Our intelligent system takes into account factors like age, weight, and dietary requirements to ensure optimal nutrition and portion control.

Smart Pet Recognition

The Hobbit Pet Feeder is equipped with advanced pet recognition technology. It can identify each pet using a combination of RFID tags or facial recognition, ensuring that the right pet receives the correct meals. No more mix-ups or overfeeding!

Healthy Portion Control

Maintaining a healthy weight is crucial for your pet's overall well-being. The Hobbit Pet Feeder provides precise portion control, preventing overeating and promoting a balanced diet. You can monitor your pet's intake and adjust portion sizes as needed, all with the touch of a button
